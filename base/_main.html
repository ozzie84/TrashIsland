The Expedition

In May 2017 Esther Roeling and Paul Niel completed the first succesful coasteering expedition of Hong Kong Island. In 6 days they climbed, swam, scrambled and hiked the entire coastline, took 51 water samples and mapped 163 trash pollution spots
